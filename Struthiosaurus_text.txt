ENG

Struthiosaurus or Ostrich Lizard was a dinosaur found in Europe (more precisely, countries like Romania, Hungary, Greece and Turkey). Also known under other names such as Crataeomus, Danubiosaurus or Pleuropeltus, it reached around 2.2 meters in length and was herbivorous. It could be found in 19 different specimens, in the Santonian-Maastrichtian period of the Cretaceous.
